Concept

Influenced has been designed as a collaborative stakeholder and communication stategy platform. It is run as a secure cloud web application. Users of the service can log in and create projects. These projects consist of issues. An issue is some challenge or political objective in which your company wishes to influence the political decisions to favour your point of view.

Users can define the key stakeholders and their current position with relation to the issues at hand. Researchers can link to background material for both the issue and the stakeholder. Plans can be defined with regards to what actions, meetings, letters, etc will be performed to try and persuade people of your way of thinking.

You can document and record when you perform actions and then update each of the stakeholders as and when their position changes. Influenced has a unqiue visual interface that allows you drag and drop stakeholders to where they are related to an issue.

While influenced shows all the key information about an issue, it also allows you to generate reports to help quantify progress, e.g. how many meetings have we had, how much has a stakeholders position changed. These kinds of insights help you work smarter and faster. In addition Influenced can generate powerpoint presentations to help summarise the current focus of activities and list out key stakeholders.

Influenced is the first digital platform to assist in the process of stakeholder mapping and communication campaign execution. It helps introduce basic workflows, measurement points and best practie processes to effectively influence stakeholders.
